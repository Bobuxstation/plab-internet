Who We Are

The South Maritimian Democratic Forces (SMDF) were born from a single, undeniable truth: no crown, no tyrant, no tradition can silence a free people. We are farmers, teachers, students, veterans, mothers, fathers, and dreamers. We believe in a Central Maritimia where every voice is sacred, and every life has value. We did not choose war — we were forced to defend the hope of a better future. Freedom is not given. It is won.
